AI-Powered Mastermind Solver

In my dissertation titled 'Implementing Artificial Intelligence in Mastermind,' the study delves into the integration of artificial intelligence for the optimization of gameplay strategies in the abstract strategy game Mastermind. This research involves extensive experimentation, examining the application of machine learning algorithms and intelligent decision-making models.

Future project aims:
The system can be enhanced through the implementation of a Graphical User Interface (GUI), which visually presents the Mastermind game.

  1. Python
  2. OpenAI Gym
  3. Stable Baselines
  4. TensorFlow
  5. NumPy

Training Performance Visualisation:
Three algorithms (PPO, A2C, and TRPO) were selected for training within the Mastermind environment, with each algorithm running for 500 million timesteps. Cumulative rewards were saved during training and then used to calculate and plot their 300-period rolling mean, providing a clear comparison of reward progression.
